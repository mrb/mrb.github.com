Music

I've been making music since the early 90's, when I figured out that my tape deck had an echo control on the microphone input, and I could use a bare wire as a sound source. I've evolved ever so slightly since then.

I have been a member of bands Double Leopards and Religious Knives, and the work I've been doing in the last few years can be found on my bandcamp page here.

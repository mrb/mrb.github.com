Venice, CA, June 14, 2021


Oceanic crucible,
cement and fracture.

Two breezes deep,
fortified and aluminized.

Alloys,
false purity,
a sense of cents,
      of calm and spray,
      freedom of enterprise.

A sleeper cell,
too asleep to sell.

Elements,
elements,
elements.
